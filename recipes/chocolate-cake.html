Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake with chocolate ganache ideal for birthdays

Ingredients :

  • 125gr of chocolate powder
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 125ml of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of boiling water

Steps :

  1. beat sugar and eggs
  2. add chocolate powder and beat a little more
  3. add flour little by little beating with a slower speed
  4. add the boiling water and beat for a few more seconds
  5. put the cake batter in a greased cake tin
  6. baked in a oven at 180 degrees celsius for 30 mins
