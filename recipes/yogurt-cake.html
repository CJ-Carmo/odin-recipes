Yogurt cake

simple nad easy iogurt cake

ingredients:



  • 4 eggs
  • 4 yogurt packaging full with flour
  • 4 yogurt packaging almost full of sugar
  • half of Yogurt packaging of oil


Steps :


  1. Put all ingredients in a bowl
  2. beat for 5 min until well incorporated
  3. put the batter in a greased and floured cake tin
  4. take to the oven for about 30 min at 180ยบ celsius
